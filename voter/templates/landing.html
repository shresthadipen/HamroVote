Designed for Students

HamroVote was developed specifically for Kathmandu College. We focus on absolute anonymity. Our decoupling algorithm ensures that while your participation is recorded, your specific choice remains a mathematical secret, known only to you.

Total Privacy

Our system separates your identity from your ballot. No one can ever link your name to your choice.

Instant Audits

No more manual counting. Results are aggregated instantly using secure background processing.

Cross-Device

Vote from your laptop at home or your smartphone in the classroom. Access anywhere, anytime.
