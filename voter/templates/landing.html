Welcome to HamroVote

This voting system is developed for Kathmandu College students to participate in student council elections securely and conveniently from anywhere.

Only registered students with valid college credentials can access the voting system. The platform ensures complete anonymity and security while maintaining transparency in the election process.

Why Choose HamroVote?

Built with students in mind, designed for modern democracy

Secure Voting

End-to-end encrypted voting system ensures your vote remains anonymous and tamper-proof.

Verified Access

Only registered students can vote, ensuring one student - one vote principle.

Instant Results

Get election results instantly after voting closes. No manual counting delays.
